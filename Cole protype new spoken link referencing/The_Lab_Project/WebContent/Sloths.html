Sloth Facts

  1. Sloths sleep between 5 and 20 hours a day.
  2. Sloths have an extra neck vertebrae which allows them to rotate their head 270 degrees.
  3. Sloths are very clumbsy on land but make excellent swimmers.
  4. There are two main types of sloths. Two-toed sloths and three-toed sloths.

Giraffe Facts

  1. A girafes heart beats up to 170times/min.
  2. Girafes have the longest tail of any land mammal - up to 8 feet long.
  3. The average life span of a giraffe is 25 years in the wild and 30 years in captivity.
  4. There are a total of 9 giraffe species.

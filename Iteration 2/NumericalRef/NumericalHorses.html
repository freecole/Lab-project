Horse Facts

  1. You measure a horse's height in hands. Each hand equals four inches.
  2. A farrier is a person who makes horse shoes and fits them on your horse.
  3. An average life span for a horse is around 20 -25 years.
  4. Camargue horses are completely white as adults. Their babies are pure black when they are born.

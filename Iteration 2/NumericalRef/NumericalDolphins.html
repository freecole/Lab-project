Dolphin Facts

  1. Dolphins can swim up to 260 m below the surface of the ocean.
  2. The largest dolphin is the killer whale(also known as Orca).
  3. Dolphins can stay up to 15 minutes under water.
  4. There are 36 different kinds of ocean dolphins and 5 species of river dolphins.
